About Us

Nickin Game Studios (trading as Nickin) is a game development studio on the Roblox platform, focused on creating high-quality, fun, and accessible gaming experiences for players of all ages. The studio was founded on October 31, 2025, by Saransak L. and Theeraphat P., with the vision of developing games that go beyond pure entertainment, placing strong emphasis on creativity, detailed game worlds, and player experience.

The studio prioritizes intuitive yet deep gameplay design, delivering content that resonates with both Thai players and a global audience. By blending unique identity, creative ideas, and Roblox technology seamlessly, Nickin Game Studios aims to create games that are high in quality and sustainable in the long term.

Nickin Game Studios is committed to continuous development and regular updates, actively listening to feedback from the player community to improve and expand its games over time. The studio’s goal is to grow into a small but outstanding game development studio, recognized within the Roblox game development community.
